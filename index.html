Plot Synopsis

Hoping to win easy money, a broke and desperate Gi-hun agrees to take part in an enigmatic game with other participants who have also failed at life for various reasons. Each is hopeful to win the more than 38 million US dollar prize, but not long into the first round, unforeseen horrors unfold. Set on an isolated island the participants are locked up until there is a final winner. The story incorporates popular Korean children's games from the 1970s and 1980s, such as squid game, but not all games are innocent...

Staring: Lee Jung-jar, Park Hae-soo , Wi Ha-jun
Creators: Hwang Dong-hyuk
